> Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing campaign to understand customer behavior and identify factors influencing campaign conversion.

An interactive Power BI dashboard was built to visualize customer response patterns and improve marketing decision-making.

> Key Steps

Data cleaning and preparation in Excel / Power BI
Created KPI: Conversion Rate (Yes / Total Customers)
Customer segmentation by job category
Analysis of campaign performance by contact channel
Built interactive Power BI dashboard with multiple visuals
 Visualizations
Campaign response distribution (Yes / No)
Conversion rate by job category
Campaign performance by contact channel


> Key Insights

Students and retired customers showed the highest conversion rates
Cellular communication channel performed better than telephone
Overall campaign conversion rate was relatively low (~12%)


> Project Goal

To support data-driven marketing decisions by identifying high-value customer segments and effective communication channels.
